Beagle Beagle is a playful and energetic hound. It has, in general, height of 1 foot, and weights in general from 18 to 30 pounds. Beagle is very known for exercising and all-around friendliness. One of the thing that distinguishes Beagle from other breeds is that it does not ‘yap’ so to speak. It growls and howls, mostly in the morning to wake people up.Source: dogtime.com
Bichon Frise Bichon Frise is a lovely and charming companion. In general, it has a height from 9 inches to 11 inches, weighs from 7 to 12 pounds. It is known for adaptability and all-around friendliness. Bichon Frise is also known to have a doll-like face with white fluffy hair. Many are appealed by its look in addition to its quirky and bright personality. Source: dogtime.com
Dashund Daschund is often named to be the wiener dog. It is the smallest of the hounds family. In general, it has a height of 8 inches to 9 inches, and weighs 16 to 32 pounds. Daschund is also known for friendliness, trainability, and scent. It is recognized to be a “scent” hound, bred to ‘hunt badgers and other tunneling animals, rabbits, and foxes’. Source: dogtime.com
Pomeranian Pomeranian is a very excited, small, yet somewhat ‘cocky’ friend. In general, it has a height from 7 inches to 1 foot, weighs 3 to 7 pounds. It had similar breed characteristics as Dashund in that it is friendly all around and adapts well. In contrast to other breeds, Pomeranian is from sled dog breed, and thus is very quick and agile. It is very subservient and good at being a nice companion to any family. Source: dogtime.com
